About Galgotias MUN

Galgotias MUN was born from the collective curiosity and shared vision of a diverse group of twelve scholars in 2015. It was our typical Genesian student driven response to the prevailing trend of materialism and aloofness from political life felt by the younger generation. Since then our conferences have been designed to create a unique learning environment that continues to counter these trends and enable a deeper understanding of the world.

About Galgotias University

Galgotias University is gearing up to host an exhilarating Model United Nations (MUN) event, set to ignite the minds of students across disciplines. This eagerly anticipated gathering will provide a dynamic platform for participants to emulate the United Nations' proceedings, delving into pivotal global issues and crafting innovative solutions. With enthusiastic delegates representing diverse viewpoints, the event promises to foster invaluable skills in diplomacy, negotiation, and critical thinking. Through spirited committee sessions, delegates will navigate intricate geopolitical landscapes, forging alliances and drafting resolutions that address pressing global challenges.
